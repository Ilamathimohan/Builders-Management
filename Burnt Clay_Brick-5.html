Clay brick is a product of mother earth, and after use, can be crushed and returned to earth, or recycled. Not only do recycled clay bricks contribute towards embodied energy savings, they also extend the life cycle of this material. Clay bricks vary in compressive strength due to the differing qualities of raw materials and the method of firing. The compressive strengths of clay bricks can vary from 3.5MPa for NFP, to greater than 50MPa for face brick extra.
